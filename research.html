Mental Health Illnesses

Mental illnesses can impact a persons state of mind, the way they feel, act, and think are affected. Examples of some mental illnesses are depression, eating disorders, obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD) , and Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) to name a few.
